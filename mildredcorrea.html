Mildred Correa

Biography

Life is a process in which we are constantly learning through experiences and involvement. I seek opportunities that will better my future academically, personally, and professionally. I have received my AA in Communication Arts at Pasadena City College and am currently attending Humboldt State University where I am majoring in Graphic Design and pursuing a Certificate in Museum & Gallery Practices. Through several internships and freelance projects for non-profits and schools, I have had the privilege of working alongside inspirational people who strive to change the world for the better.

Graphic design is much more than a means of making “cool” fliers, it is a way of communicating an organization’s or individual's message in the hopes of bringing awareness to a specific event or service that will hopefully strengthen the community. I plan to use my skills to create a positive change on earth by working within an organization or individual whose efforts goes towards benefiting the community; whether through art, music, activism, education, or some other manner. Although design is my passion, I have many interests including writing, readings, and especially creating DIY crafts out of upcycled materials.
