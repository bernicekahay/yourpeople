Biography

Keisha Keisha is a singer, song writer, published writer, actress, poet, dancer, print model and motivational speaker. Keisha Keisha began singing in the choir and acting in church plays at her grandfather church in oakland,California at the age of 3years old. As she grew up she began to feel more confident in her craft, so she began writing, cherographing dances and directing Theater plays at her grandfather church. When she Keisha Keisha started high school, her two favorite subjects were English and History. Whereas she fell in love with writing controversial poems and having her poems published in a book called the "Clatter" that was created by her fellow peers. The Clatter poetry book was entered every year to the scholastic high-school book contest and won 1st place for 4 years straight. Keisha Keisha was very competitive in her craft. What made Keisha Keisha want to become a motivational speaker was when she lost her brother to tragic event in 2009, Her mother was diagnosed with breast cancer a year later, then a year in a half after that her grandmother died of stage 4 pancreatic cancer. Those back yo back tradigies shifted the way she seen life. She struggled for quite some time to find her inner strength but long behold she found it with help of God and herself. That is the reason she has became a motivational speaker because she feels the need to help people love themselves and live your life to the fullest. After high school, Keisha Keisha decided to move to pasadena,ca to attend a music program at California college of music for 1 year. She learned how to write her own music, read music, stage presence, and ear training. She then started attending pasadena city College where she is currently attending focusing on her degree in communications. As of now Keisha Keisha has done a few Theater plays, and a short film that was shown at the Universal studios theater. She has done some modeling print photoshoots. Keisha Keisha loves to stat productive doing the things she love. Keisha Keisha is currently working on her first EP album which is untitled but she has 1 song out that she is featured on called "The Knowledge". The knowledge record is available to listen to on soundcloud. Keisha Keisha is definitely into fashion and technology. She hopes to collab with other designers and create her own clothing and shoes fashion line for women, men and children. Her dream is definitely collab with samsung one day to create her own cellphone and headphones devices. Keisha Keisha resides in the greater Los Angeles, Ca and she is still believing in herself that all of her dreams can come true if she keeps the faith, pray, focus, stay discipline, and have determination no matter what.
