Riley Bathauer

Biography

I am a full time student at UC Berkeley and a photographer/videographer. Photography allows me to produce images of the world around me from many perspectives. It offers a chance to tell stories and to define the moments we deem worthy of immortalization. As a photographer I get the chance to create snapshots of people's moments, of their lives and stories, and have fun in the process.

I am studying Business / Media and love the wilderness (especially Mammoth). Along with photography I also am expanding my videography services. I can work alone or with a small team on a variety of projects: event videography, weddings, wedding trailers, product advertisements, music videos, company pitches, and more. I can write, direct, record, and edit videos.

I am greatful to work in a field that lets me work with people. Above all photos and videos are fun and my biggest goal (apart from awesome content) is to make sure the process is fun and everyone feels great about their shoot.
I look forward to working with you.

-Riley
