Biography

Christopher Vinan is a Media Studies and Sociology student at the University of California, Berkeley. He has lived in Philadelphia, Los Angeles, and inside of a womb, which has given Chris the different perspectives needed to create creative creations. Chris has always pursued video production but also partakes in the following: writing (short-stories/full-length films/television pilots/ academic theories/ music/ poetry), graphic design, photography, stand-up comedy, acting, and painting. Chris is 20 years old and filled with optimism, blood, and water. Chris realizes that creation is what makes humanity unique and that within the human race there are those that create and those who enjoy what is created. Chris would like to be the former. Chris is also uncomfortable typing about himself in the third person, but he knows that he must make sacrifices to get what he needs. And if you are reading this sentence, then Chris needs what you are offering.
