Michael Sears

Biography

Most of my artistic style is influenced by the psychedelic rock concert posters of the fillmore auditorium in san francisco during the late 1960s, particularly Wes Wilson, he is my ultimate, all time single artist who inspires me. I also gain a lot of passion and influence from my emotions, especially depression and anger, as well as from my past substance abuse. A lot of my work that I have sampled was created during times of extreme emotion where I felt the only way to release the tension I feel mentally was to express it with a pen and a paper. Thank you for checking my artwork out and message me for any questions!

Love&Peace,
Michael Scott Sears(The Acid Prince)
